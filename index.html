Quality Grades

A+ Excellent

  • Minimal to no signs of previous use
  • Battery health typically 85%+
  • All features fully functional

A Good

  • Light scratches or minor cosmetic wear
  • Battery health typically 80%+
  • All features fully functional
  • 14-day limited warranty included

ASIS As-Is

  • Visible cosmetic damage (cracks, dents)
  • May have functional issues (clearly stated)
  • Fully disclosed before purchase
  • No warranty — priced accordingly
All devices are tested for core functionality regardless of grade.
